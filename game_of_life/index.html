Rules

This is a copy of Conway's Game of Life. In this game, each spot can be in two states: alive or dead. An alive spot can stay alive if it has two or three neighbors. An alive spot can become dead if it has more than three neighbors or less than two neighbors. A dead spot can become alive if it has three neighbors. You can select spots on the board by clicking or by clicking and dragging. To start the simulation, press the play button. To stop the simulation, press the pause button. To go back to the previous state, press the rewind button.
